That Day

May 19th, 2025, the day I finally asked you to be mine, that day in the evening when you told me that we could meet, I knew in that moment this day was going to be special, I didn't know what it was that I was going to do, but I knew for a fact that the day we met right after us having confessed to each other would be a special day, Having fought my mother to let me go see the love of my life, I ran towards your house scared that you might not be able to come because of the rain but the moment I saw you there, in front of you gate just standing a smiling at me, I knew for a fact that you were never going to let me down ever. It rained quite heavily that evening, I knew that day that it was going to be a long time before I get to meet you again, Soo I wanted to make sure that I do not miss out of the things that I had made a checklist of in my mind. Although I almost forgot all about it when you hugged me, but I managed to squeeze in the "will you be my girlfriend" and most importantly our first kiss as well. I had never been happier, I went back home incredibly content and happy spent the entire night chatting with you, it was beautiful.
